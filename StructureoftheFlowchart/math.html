Basic Mathematics for Software Engineering

Mathematics is a cornerstone of software engineering. Mastering algebra, geometry, and logic will help you understand programming concepts and algorithms.

Topics to Focus On

  • Algebra: Solve equations, understand variables, and linear functions.
  • Geometry: Understand shapes, angles, and coordinate systems.
  • Probability: Learn basic statistics and probabilities.

Resources

  • Khan Academy - Mathematics
  • Brilliant.org - Math and Logic

Examples

Example 1: Solve for x: 2x + 5 = 15

Solution: Subtract 5 from both sides, then divide by 2: x = 5

Example 2: Find the area of a triangle with a base of 10 cm and a height of 5 cm.

Solution: Area = 1/2 × base × height = 1/2 × 10 × 5 = 25 cm²
