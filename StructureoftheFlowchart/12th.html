12th Grade Roadmap: Steps to Becoming a Software Engineer

Here’s what you need to focus on in Class 12th to excel in software engineering.

Advanced Mathematics

  • Master Calculus (Derivatives and Integrals)
  • Learn Matrix Algebra
  • Understand Probability and Combinatorics

Resources: Khan Academy, NCERT Textbooks, and Brilliant.org

Intermediate Programming

  • Learn OOP Concepts (Java, Python, or C++)
  • Write Small Programs (e.g., Sorting, Searching)
  • Build Mini Projects (e.g., Expense Tracker)

Resources: GeeksforGeeks, Codecademy, and W3Schools

Entrance Exam Prep

  • Prepare for Competitive Exams (JEE, CET)
  • Focus on Logical Reasoning & Quantitative Aptitude
  • Practice Past Year Papers and Mock Tests

Resources: Embibe, Aakash BYJU’s, and Allen
